i love me some hot dog

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal and flower
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt and pepper
  • milk
  • egg
  • oil
  • beef frankfurters

instructions

  1. make the batter by combining the dry Ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and the roll in the batter
  3. fry the corndogs in the hot oil until lightly browned
